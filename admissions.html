Fee Structure

A transparent overview of fees and tuition for the upcoming academic session.

Academic Grade Registration Fee (One-Time) Tuition Fee (Per Term) Activity & Tech Fee (Annual)
Primary School (Grades 1-5) $200 $3,500 $400
Middle School (Grades 6-8) $250 $4,200 $500
High School (Grades 9-12) $300 $5,500 $650
Please Note:
  • Tuition fees are payable in 3 installments (one per term) before the commencement of each term.
  • A sibling discount of 10% on tuition is applicable for the second and subsequent child enrolled at Aura Crest.
  • Fees cover smart classroom tools, laboratory access, and general extra-curricular club materials. External exam and uniforms are charged separately.
