Curriculum Levels

We structure learning stages to nurture appropriate skills, values, and academic milestones at every age group.

Primary School

Grades 1 - 5

Focuses on core literacy, numeracy, creative expression, and cultivating positive social behaviors.

  • Foundational Mathematics
  • English Language & Creative Writing
  • Environmental Studies & Basic Science
  • Art, Craft & General Music
  • Physical Education & Basic Athletics

Middle School

Grades 6 - 8

Encourages independent thinking, complex problem solving, and analytical study methods.

  • Pre-Algebra & Integrated Geometry
  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology Intro
  • World History & Geography
  • Modern Foreign Languages
  • ICT & Computer Programming Basics

High School

Grades 9 - 12

Rigorous preparation for university admissions, standardized testing, and professional careers.

  • AP Calculus & Advanced Statistics
  • Advanced Lab Sciences (Physics/Chem)
  • Economics & Global Politics
  • English Literature & Rhetoric
  • University Prep Seminars

Our Campus Facilities

Providing students with first-rate resources to research, experiment, compete, and excel.

Scientific Laboratories

Well-equipped experimental zones hosting advanced modules for molecular biology, organic chemistry, and robotic engineering projects.

Smart Media Library

A digitalized catalog featuring 20,000+ print titles, research terminals, subscription-based journals, and dedicated quiet study cells.

Athletics Stadium

Home of the Aura Crest Wolves, hosting an all-weather football pitch, a professional athletic track, basketball courts, and an indoor gym.
