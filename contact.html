Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding administration, transport, uniforms, and student life.

What are the school operating hours?

Regular classes operate from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extra-curricular clubs and coaching sessions run from 3:15 PM to 4:30 PM.

Is school transport facility available?

Yes, Aura Crest Academy operates a fleet of air-conditioned school buses that cover all major neighborhoods and sub-districts in Crestwood City. Bus routes, timetables, and quarterly bus pass fees are available upon request from the administration department.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer Academic Merit Scholarships and Sports Excellence Scholarships for High School students (Grades 9-12). Applications open during the month of March each year. Need-based financial aid is also evaluated on a case-by-case basis by the board.

What is the school policy on uniforms?

All enrolled students must wear the designated school uniform. Uniform kits consist of formal blazers (for assemblies), dress shirts, trousers or pleated skirts, and formal leather shoes. Physical Education kits are worn on days containing sports sessions. Catalogues and supplier details are handed to parents on enrollment completion.
